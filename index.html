Great to see you here! I am a 5th year PhD student in Computer Science at UCLA. My research focuses on using machine learning to help solve healthcare problems. Before UCLA, I completed my Bachelor and Master in engineering at Tsinghua University.

I am currently co-advised by Prof. Cho-Jui Hsieh and Prof. Joseph Kim. I have previously worked with Prof. Hua Wei and Prof. Ayça Ayoub (UCLA), Prof. Yuan Luo (Northwestern), and Prof. Reza Zanjirani Farahani (University of Tehran).

I am the first author and main contributor of Tianshou and EnvPool with 8k+ and 5k+ stars on Github. Please feel free to reach out if you are interested in reinforcement learning and its applications!

Experience

I am a research intern at Google DeepMind. I worked on training language models at scale and their data pipeline during summer 2024, based in the Mountain View office.

During summer 2023, I worked on reinforcement learning and off-policy evaluation at Amazon (AWS AI Lab).

During summer 2021, I worked at Amazon (Alexa AI) for dialog system simulations using reinforcement learning.

During summer 2019, I worked at NVIDIA for simulated training of robot arms with reinforcement learning.

Prior to that, I was the founding engineer of Blab AI (acquired by Clinc in 2019).

I am the first author and main contributor of:

  • Tianshou, a reinforcement learning library. It provides a concise API and has been used by 4k+ GitHub users and has 8k+ stars. It was officially used in the 2020-2021 NeurIPS competition and IJCAI competition.
  • EnvPool, a reinforcement learning simulation accelerator that provides 10x-100x improvements on CPU and GPU environments.
